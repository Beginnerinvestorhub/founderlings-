# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in Beginner Investor Hub a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, abusive or offensive comments
- Public or private intimidation
- Disrespectful language or behavior

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to [your email here]. All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly.
